Meeting Notes — Requirements Workshop
Date: 13 Apr 2026
Project: LES CRM Implementation
Attendees: Project Manager, Business Analyst, Sales Head, IT Manager

Key Requirements Discussed:

1. Lead Management
   - Capture leads from website, email, and phone calls
   - Assign leads automatically to sales reps based on region
   - Track lead status: New, Contacted, Qualified, Converted, Lost
   - Set follow-up reminders for each lead

2. Opportunity Management
   - Convert qualified leads to opportunities
   - Track opportunity stages: Discovery, Proposal, Negotiation, Closed Won, Closed Lost
   - Attach quotes and proposals to opportunities
   - Forecast revenue based on pipeline

3. Account & Contact Management
   - Maintain 360-degree view of each customer account
   - Link multiple contacts to a single account
   - Track interaction history (calls, emails, meetings)
   - Store account hierarchy (parent-child relationships)

4. Reporting & Dashboard
   - Sales manager needs daily pipeline report
   - Weekly activity report per sales rep
   - Monthly revenue forecast vs actual
   - Real-time dashboard for KPIs

5. Integration Requirements
   - Sync with Outlook for email and calendar
   - Import existing data from Excel spreadsheets
   - Export reports to PDF and Excel

Priorities agreed:
- High: Lead capture, Lead assignment, Opportunity tracking
- Medium: Reporting dashboard, Outlook integration
- Low: Advanced analytics, Mobile app

Timeline: Go-live target is Q3 2026
Budget: Approved for Phase 1 implementation
